TERM LOAN CREDIT AGREEMENT

This Term Loan Credit Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of December 10, 2025, between GLOBAL MANUFACTURING CORP, a Delaware corporation (the "Borrower"), and the lenders party hereto (the "Lenders").

ARTICLE I - CREDIT FACILITY

The Lenders agree to provide a term loan facility in the aggregate principal amount of SEVEN HUNDRED FIFTY MILLION DOLLARS ($750,000,000) denominated in United States Dollars (USD) for the acquisition of Industrial Components Ltd.

ARTICLE II - INTEREST RATE AND FEES

Interest Rate: Term SOFR plus 4.50% per annum, subject to adjustment based on leverage ratio:

Leverage-Based Pricing Grid:
- Total Leverage Ratio ≥ 5.00x: SOFR + 4.50%
- Total Leverage Ratio < 5.00x but ≥ 4.50x: SOFR + 4.25%
- Total Leverage Ratio < 4.50x but ≥ 4.00x: SOFR + 4.00%
- Total Leverage Ratio < 4.00x: SOFR + 3.75%

Upfront Fee: 1.00% of facility amount
Commitment Fee: 0.50% per annum on undrawn amounts

ARTICLE III - MATURITY AND AMORTIZATION

Maturity Date: December 10, 2032 (7 years)

Mandatory Amortization Schedule:
- Years 1-2: 5% per annum ($37,500,000 annually)
- Years 3-4: 7.5% per annum ($56,250,000 annually)
- Years 5-6: 10% per annum ($75,000,000 annually)
- Year 7: Remaining balance due at maturity

ARTICLE IV - FINANCIAL COVENANTS

The Borrower shall maintain:

Maximum Total Leverage Ratio:
- Through December 31, 2026: 5.50:1.00
- Through December 31, 2027: 5.25:1.00
- Through December 31, 2028: 5.00:1.00
- Thereafter: 4.75:1.00

Minimum Interest Coverage Ratio: 3.00:1.00
Maximum Capital Expenditures: $50,000,000 per fiscal year (excluding acquisition-related capex)

ARTICLE V - ACQUISITION TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Target Company: Industrial Components Ltd. (UK-based manufacturing company)
Purchase Price: $850,000,000 (including assumed debt)
Equity Contribution: Minimum $100,000,000 from Borrower

Conditions Precedent to Acquisition:
(a) Completion of satisfactory due diligence
(b) Receipt of all required regulatory approvals
(c) No material adverse change in target company
(d) Execution of management retention agreements
(e) Obtaining required insurance coverage

ARTICLE VI - REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Standard representations plus acquisition-specific warranties:
(a) Accuracy of all due diligence materials provided
(b) No material pending litigation affecting target company
(c) Compliance with all antitrust and competition laws
(d) Accuracy of pro forma financial projections
(e) Validity of acquisition purchase agreement

ARTICLE VII - COVENANTS

Affirmative Covenants:
(a) Integration planning and execution oversight
(b) Monthly reporting during first 12 months post-acquisition
(c) Maintenance of target company's key customer relationships
(d) Retention of target company's senior management team
(e) Achievement of specified synergy targets ($25,000,000 annually by Year 2)

Negative Covenants:
(a) No additional acquisitions exceeding $25,000,000 without consent
(b) No asset sales exceeding $50,000,000 without consent
(c) Restricted payments limited to $10,000,000 annually
(d) No changes to target company's core business operations

ARTICLE VIII - MANDATORY PREPAYMENTS

Mandatory prepayment required from:
(a) 100% of net proceeds from asset sales exceeding $10,000,000
(b) 100% of net proceeds from debt issuances
(c) 50% of excess cash flow (as defined) if leverage ratio exceeds 4.50x
(d) 100% of insurance proceeds exceeding $5,000,000

ARTICLE IX - SECURITY PACKAGE

First Priority Liens on:
- All assets of Borrower and material subsidiaries
- 100% of equity interests in target company
- All intellectual property and technology assets
- Intercompany notes and receivables

Guarantees:
- Full and unconditional guarantee by all material subsidiaries
- Upstream guarantee by target company post-acquisition

ARTICLE X - EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Standard events of default plus:
(a) Failure to complete acquisition by March 31, 2026
(b) Material breach of acquisition purchase agreement
(c) Loss of key customer contracts representing >15% of target company revenue
(d) Failure to achieve minimum synergy targets by specified dates
(e) Material environmental liability exceeding $15,000,000

ARTICLE XI - GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by New York law, with exclusive jurisdiction in New York state and federal courts.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
