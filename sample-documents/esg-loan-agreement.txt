SUSTAINABILITY-LINKED LOAN AGREEMENT

This Sustainability-Linked Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of December 20, 2025, between GREEN ENERGY SOLUTIONS INC., a Delaware corporation (the "Borrower"), and the participating lenders (the "Lenders").

ARTICLE I - CREDIT FACILITY

The Lenders agree to provide a term loan facility in the aggregate principal amount of THREE HUNDRED MILLION DOLLARS ($300,000,000) denominated in United States Dollars (USD).

ARTICLE II - INTEREST AND SUSTAINABILITY PRICING

Base Interest Rate: Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) plus 3.25% per annum

SUSTAINABILITY PERFORMANCE TARGETS AND PRICING ADJUSTMENTS:

Target 1: Carbon Emission Reduction
- Achieve 40% reduction in Scope 1 and 2 carbon emissions by December 31, 2027 (baseline: 2023 levels)
- Interest rate discount: 0.50% per annum upon achievement and maintenance

Target 2: Renewable Energy Transition
- Achieve 75% renewable energy in all operations by December 31, 2026
- Interest rate discount: 0.25% per annum upon achievement and maintenance

Target 3: ESG Rating Improvement
- Maintain ESG rating of A- or higher from MSCI or equivalent rating agency
- Interest rate discount: 0.25% per annum upon achievement and maintenance

Maximum total discount: 1.00% per annum
Minimum interest rate floor: SOFR + 1.75% per annum

ARTICLE III - MATURITY AND REPAYMENT

The facility shall mature on December 20, 2032 (7 years), with quarterly principal payments of $10,714,286 beginning March 31, 2026.

ARTICLE IV - FINANCIAL COVENANTS

The Borrower shall maintain:
- Maximum Total Leverage Ratio: 3.75:1.00
- Minimum Interest Coverage Ratio: 4.00:1.00
- Minimum Liquidity: $40,000,000

ARTICLE V - SUSTAINABILITY REPORTING AND VERIFICATION

Sustainability Reporting Requirements:
(a) Annual sustainability report within 120 days of year-end
(b) Quarterly carbon emissions data within 45 days of quarter-end
(c) Annual third-party verification of all sustainability metrics
(d) Compliance with Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD) framework

Verification Process:
- Independent third-party verification required for all sustainability targets
- Acceptable verifiers include: EY, KPMG, PwC, Deloitte, or other recognized ESG auditors
- Verification must be completed within 90 days of measurement date
- Borrower bears all verification costs

ARTICLE VI - ESG COMMITMENTS

The Borrower commits to:
(a) Implementing comprehensive environmental management system
(b) Publishing annual sustainability report aligned with GRI Standards
(c) Setting science-based targets for carbon reduction
(d) Achieving zero waste to landfill by December 31, 2028
(e) Maintaining diverse and inclusive workforce (minimum 40% women in leadership by 2027)

ARTICLE VII - USE OF PROCEEDS

Proceeds shall be used exclusively for:
- Renewable energy infrastructure development (minimum 60%)
- Energy efficiency improvements (maximum 25%)
- Sustainable technology research and development (maximum 15%)

Green Use of Proceeds Reporting:
- Annual allocation and impact reporting required
- Compliance with Green Loan Principles (LMA/APLMA/LSTA)
- External review by qualified provider

ARTICLE VIII - SUSTAINABILITY COVENANT BREACHES

Failure to achieve sustainability targets will result in:
- Loss of applicable interest rate discount
- Requirement to engage sustainability consultant (at Borrower's expense)
- Enhanced reporting requirements
- Potential acceleration of facility if material ESG deterioration occurs

ARTICLE IX - REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Standard representations plus:
(a) No material environmental liabilities or violations
(b) Compliance with all applicable environmental laws
(c) Accuracy of all sustainability data and metrics provided
(d) Commitment to continuous improvement in ESG performance

ARTICLE X - SECURITY AND GUARANTEES

Security Package:
- First lien on all renewable energy assets
- Pledge of green technology intellectual property
- Environmental insurance policy (minimum $25,000,000)
- Step-down provisions upon achieving investment grade rating

ARTICLE XI - GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York, with disputes resolved through binding arbitration.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
