COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE CONSTRUCTION LOAN AGREEMENT

This Construction Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of December 5, 2025, between PROPERTY DEVELOPMENT LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (the "Borrower"), and the lenders party hereto (the "Lenders").

ARTICLE I - CONSTRUCTION LOAN FACILITY

The Lenders agree to provide a construction-to-permanent loan facility in the aggregate principal amount of TWO HUNDRED MILLION DOLLARS ($200,000,000) denominated in United States Dollars (USD) for the development of Metropolitan Office Complex.

ARTICLE II - PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Project: Metropolitan Office Complex
Location: 1500 Corporate Boulevard, Austin, Texas
Description: 850,000 square foot Class A office building with retail component
Total Project Cost: $240,000,000
Borrower Equity: $40,000,000 (minimum 16.7%)

ARTICLE III - INTEREST RATE AND FEES

Construction Phase Interest Rate: Prime Rate plus 1.75% per annum
Permanent Phase Interest Rate: 10-Year Treasury Rate plus 2.25% per annum

Fees:
- Loan Origination Fee: 1.25% of facility amount
- Inspection Fee: $2,500 per inspection
- Legal and Administrative Fees: $75,000

ARTICLE IV - LOAN PHASES

Phase 1: Construction Phase (24 months maximum)
- Interest-only payments monthly
- Funds advanced based on construction progress
- Professional cost certification required for each draw

Phase 2: Permanent Phase (25 years amortization)
- Principal and interest payments monthly
- Conversion automatic upon completion and stabilization
- Minimum debt service coverage ratio: 1.25x

ARTICLE V - CONSTRUCTION DRAW PROCEDURES

Draw Requirements:
(a) AIA Form G702/G703 Application and Certificate for Payment
(b) Architect's certificate of work completion
(c) General contractor's sworn statement and lien waivers
(d) Borrower's certificate of compliance with loan covenants
(e) Title company endorsement (for draws exceeding $5,000,000)

Maximum Draw Frequency: Monthly
Minimum Draw Amount: $1,000,000
Retainage: 10% until 50% completion, then 5%

ARTICLE VI - FINANCIAL COVENANTS

During Construction Phase:
- Minimum liquidity: $15,000,000
- Maximum loan-to-cost ratio: 83.3%
- Completion guarantee from principals

During Permanent Phase:
- Minimum debt service coverage ratio: 1.25x
- Maximum loan-to-value ratio: 75%
- Minimum occupancy rate: 85% (after stabilization period)

ARTICLE VII - CONSTRUCTION REQUIREMENTS

General Contractor: Must be approved by Lender
- Minimum 10 years experience in similar projects
- Bonding capacity of at least $250,000,000
- Payment and performance bonds required (100% of contract amount)

Architect: Must be licensed and experienced in commercial office development
Construction Contract: Guaranteed maximum price contract required
Permits: All required permits must be obtained before first draw

ARTICLE VIII - LEASING REQUIREMENTS

Pre-Leasing Requirements:
- Minimum 40% pre-leased before conversion to permanent loan
- Average lease term minimum 7 years
- Tenant credit quality acceptable to Lender
- No single tenant to exceed 25% of net rentable area

Property Management:
- Professional property management company required
- Management agreement subject to Lender approval
- Monthly operating statements required

ARTICLE IX - INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Required Insurance Coverage:
- Builder's risk insurance: Full replacement cost
- General liability: $5,000,000 per occurrence
- Professional liability: $2,000,000 (architect and engineer)
- Environmental liability: $10,000,000
- Flood insurance: If applicable per FEMA maps

All policies must name Lender as additional insured and loss payee.

ARTICLE X - ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

Environmental Requirements:
(a) Phase I Environmental Site Assessment completed and acceptable
(b) No known environmental contamination
(c) Compliance with all applicable environmental laws
(d) LEED Silver certification minimum required
(e) Environmental compliance certificate at completion

ARTICLE XI - COMPLETION AND STABILIZATION

Completion Requirements:
- Certificate of occupancy obtained
- All construction substantially complete per plans and specifications
- All mechanical systems operational and warranted
- Parking facilities completed and operational
- Landscaping and site work completed

Stabilization Requirements:
- Minimum 85% occupancy achieved
- Debt service coverage ratio of 1.25x demonstrated for 3 consecutive months
- All tenant improvement work completed
- Property management systems fully operational

ARTICLE XII - SECURITY AND COLLATERAL

First Mortgage Lien on:
- Real property and all improvements
- All rents, leases, and profits from the property
- All insurance proceeds and condemnation awards
- Construction contracts and architect agreements
- All permits, licenses, and approvals

Additional Security:
- Assignment of all construction contracts
- Assignment of architect and engineer agreements
- UCC-1 financing statements on all personal property
- Environmental indemnity from principals

ARTICLE XIII - EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Construction-Specific Defaults:
(a) Failure to commence construction within 90 days
(b) Work stoppage exceeding 30 consecutive days
(c) Failure to achieve substantial completion within 30 months
(d) Cost overruns exceeding 10% without additional equity
(e) Loss of required permits or approvals
(f) Contractor default or abandonment

ARTICLE XIV - GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.
